Areas

Divide to Conquer

Mechanics

The guys and ladies who develop the robot structure, 3D model the digital project and create solutions to let the machine resistent, efficient and ferocious.

Eletronic

Give live to the robot and manage the structure assembly, batteries, pneumatics and wireless communication with their knowledge on Circuits, Digital and Analogic Electronics, Motors and drive train.

Marketing

The artists who take care of social platforms, documents, team image, presentations, media and sponsors contact, partnerships and more...
